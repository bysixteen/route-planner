Iller Valley – TBD
Days 5–6 · Tue 21 Jul → Thu 23 Jul · 2 nights

Why here? The Iller valley sits between the Swabian Alb and the Allgäu foothills, south of Ulm. The best-rated campsites in this corridor are lakeside/riverside sites in the Iller valley — significantly nicer than the A8 roadside options further north. Drive from Luxembourg via A6/A8/A7 is 4h50m–5h36m depending on site. Break in Saarbrücken (2h in) makes the longer drives very manageable. Onward to Melk (Stop 5) is ~4h46m via A8 → Munich → Salzburg → A1. 2-night stop with a rest day for day trips.
⚠️ Blautopf closed: The famous blue spring in Blaubeuren is closed for renovation until end of 2028. Still worth visiting the town — but the main attraction is off-limits.
