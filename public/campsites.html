Camping Grimbergen · ~€40/night · campinggrimbergen.be

Option C — Hotel night in the city

Park Dexter free at Delta P+R — no height barriers, Metro Line 5 direct into the centre (~20 min). Sleep in a proper hotel.

Good picks for 4 people:

  • Hotel Agora Brussels Grand Place ★★★★ — quadruple rooms, 1 block from Grand Place
  • Novotel Brussels City Centre — family rooms for 4, 5 min walk to Grand Place, reliable
  • Hilton Brussels Grand Place ★★★★½ — treat yourselves option
✓ Best city experience — stay central, explore morning too before driving to Calais ✓ Dexter parked free at Delta P+R (no height limit, Metro Line 5) ✓ No late-night metro back to a campsite — just walk to bed
✗ Most expensive — family room ~€150–250 in July ✗ Check Delta P+R has space for Dexter's size before committing
