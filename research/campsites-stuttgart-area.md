## Recommendation

**For a family of 4 in a motorhome, 1 night on 21 July, driving Luxembourg → Innsbruck:**

### Top Pick: Camping Aichelberg (#1)
- Proper campsite with full facilities (showers, playground, pool, restaurant)
- Kids can swim and play after a long drive
- Electricity included free in pitch price
- Directly off the A8 — zero diversion from your route
- Good reviews (4.0/5), family-run, clean modern bathrooms
- Open 21 July (season: 17 Apr – 5 Oct)
- Only 10 km from Göppingen, in the scenic Fils Valley
- Book by email from January: mail@camping-aichelberg.de

### Runner-up: Stellplatz Herrenberg (#3)
- If you want something cheaper and don't mind a Stellplatz
- Outdoor pool next door for the kids
- Pretty old town to walk around
- Easy A81 access — slight detour south of Stuttgart but still workable
- Only 10 spaces, no showers on site

### Route note
Coming from Luxembourg on the A6, you would join the A81 southbound (passing Ludwigsburg and Stuttgart) then continue to the A8 eastbound toward Innsbruck. Both Herrenberg (A81) and Aichelberg/Weilheim (A8) are directly on this route with zero diversion.
