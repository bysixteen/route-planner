Constraints Discovered

  • Mapbox via-France routing: Mapbox routes Luxembourg → Lindau via Strasbourg/Alsace, not via Saarland/Stuttgart, producing ~6.5h. Positions east of ~9.2°E (east of Stuttgart) force the faster A6/A8 motorway corridor.
  • Esch-sur-Sûre ≠ Luxembourg City: Camping Bissen is in the far north of Luxembourg near the Belgian border [5.9537, 49.8714]. Use these coords for drive-time estimates, not Luxembourg City (~40km further south-east).
  • Old Stop 5 coords were in the Austrian Alps: [12.7939, 47.2959] is Radstadt, a ski resort — mountain road territory. The new Wachau position [15.33, 48.22] is on the Danube A1 motorway (all-motorway routing).
